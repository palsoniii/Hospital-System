Importance of Elections

Elections are a vital part of democracy. They ensure that the people have the power to elect their leaders, shaping the future of the country. In India, elections are held at various levels, and every citizen has the right to participate in the electoral process.

Voting is not only a right but a responsibility. Every citizen must exercise their vote to contribute to a better future for India.
